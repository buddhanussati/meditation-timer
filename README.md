# Meditation Timer (Anapanasati)

A minimalist, web-based meditation companion designed to guide practitioners through the 16 stages of Anapanasati (Mindfulness of Breathing). This application features a visual breathing guide, customizable timers, and interval bells.

## âœ¨ Features

* **Visual Breath Pacer:** A gentle, expanding and contracting circle animation that synchronizes with your breathing rhythm.
* **Guided Stages:** Includes the full 16 stages of Anapanasati (plus preparation), displaying specific mindfulness prompts for In-breath and Out-breath.
* **Manual Stage Control:** Easily move forward or backward through the meditation stages using on-screen controls.
* **Customizable Settings:**
    * **Breath Cycle:** Adjust the length of a full breath (e.g., 10s, 12s, or custom) to match your natural rhythm.
    * **Interval Bell:** Set a recurring mindfulness bell (e.g., every 5 mins) to keep you focused.
    * **Audio Selection:** Choose from multiple bell sounds (Beep, Water Flow, various Bells).
* **Preferences Saved:** Your settings (breath speed, bell interval, preferred sound) are saved locally in your browser, so you don't have to reset them every session.
* **Responsive Design:** Works beautifully on desktop and mobile devices.
